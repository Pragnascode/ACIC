About Us

About us

Situated at Hyderabad's MLR Institute of Technology, ACIC MLRIT symbolizes a dedication to holistic education. It was founded with the inspiring guidance of Mr. Marri Laxman Reddy and combines education, practical skills, and athletics. With the focus on grassroots innovation, ACIC MLRIT gives people at the bottom of the pyramid a platform to innovate and make significant contributions. ACIC MLRIT is committed to providing a comprehensive learning experience and is a catalyst for innovative ideas that address local issues and advance society development. It does this by emphasizing skill development, mentorship, and community engagement.
