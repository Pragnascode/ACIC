OUR MISSION

The mission of ACIC MLRIT is to enable people at the local level by providing a nurturing atmosphere for creativity, skill-building, and community involvement. We are dedicated to giving prospective business owners all the help they need, including resources and mentorship. We aim to foster concepts that not only tackle regional issues but also support the advancement of a sustainable society. We seek to promote inclusivity, creativity, and excellence in transforming novel concepts into practical solutions through cooperative efforts with industry and community partners, leaving a long-lasting positive impact on both individuals and communities.
